About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer at General Dynamics Electric Boat, contributing to the design and analysis of critical submarine systems for the Columbia Class. My work spans structural and shock analyses, procurement coordination, and system design for submarine escape hatches, rescue systems, and watertight doors, ensuring compliance with the Navy's demanding performance and safety standards in extreme underwater environments.

I bring a strong foundation in solid mechanics, mechatronics, and system dynamics, developed through both industry and research. At Northwestern University, I earned my B.S. and M.S. in Mechanical Engineering, focusing on robotics, advanced mechatronics, and manufacturing.
